# 📊 HR Workforce Attrition Analysis

**Domain:** HR Analytics + Data Analytics + Business Intelligence + Workforce Intelligence

---

# 1. Problem Statement

Employee attrition leads to increased hiring costs, productivity loss, knowledge gaps, team instability, and reduced employee morale. HR leaders require data-driven insights to understand why employees leave, identify high-risk groups, and develop effective retention strategies. This project aims to analyze workforce behavior and build an interactive dashboard that supports informed HR decision-making.

---

# 2. Dataset Description

- **Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset  
- **Total Employees:** 1,470  
- **Total Features:** 50 Columns  
- **Format:** CSV  
- **Derived Features:** Salary Band, Experience Group, Retention Risk  

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| Age | Employee age |
| Gender | Employee gender |
| Department | Department of work |
| JobRole | Employee job role |
| MonthlyIncome | Monthly salary |
| TotalWorkingYears | Overall work experience |
| WorkLifeBalance | Work-life balance rating |
| JobSatisfaction | Job satisfaction score |
| OverTime | Overtime status |
| Attrition | Employee attrition (Yes/No) |

---

# 3. KPI Definitions

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Employees | Total number of employees |
| Attrition Rate | Percentage of employees who left the company |
| Employee Retention Rate | Percentage of employees retained |
| Average Employee Tenure | Average years of service |
| Job Satisfaction Score | Average employee satisfaction rating |
| Average Income | Mean monthly income of employees |
| Overtime Percentage | Percentage of employees working overtime |
| High Risk Employee Count | Employees classified as high attrition risk |

# 5. Key Findings

- Sales and Research & Development exhibit the highest attrition.
- Employees with low satisfaction and poor work-life balance are more likely to leave.
- Overtime is strongly associated with higher attrition risk.
- Early-career employees show greater turnover tendencies.
- High-risk employees require targeted retention efforts.

---

# 6. Recommendations

- Improve work-life balance initiatives and reduce excessive overtime.
- Strengthen employee engagement and recognition programs.
- Provide clear career growth opportunities for early-career employees.
- Monitor high-risk employees proactively using dashboard insights.
- Conduct periodic satisfaction surveys to address concerns promptly.

---

# 7. Future Scope

- Develop machine learning models for attrition prediction.
- Integrate the dashboard with live HR systems.
- Incorporate employee sentiment analysis from surveys.
- Enable automated alerts for emerging retention risks.
